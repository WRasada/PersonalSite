Work in Progress

Currently, as it stands, this blog does not have a database or any real easy way for me to add posts. This is just to get something up and running until I build an official blogging app. So it's definitely not ideal and I should have the final one up and running within a couple weeks. Until then, i'll just be posting by adding a new post in the HTML and then redeploying the website. Fun stuff!
